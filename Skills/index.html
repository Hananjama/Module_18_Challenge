My Skills

User Research

I'm a UX researcher with a deeply empathetic approach to user research. I use surveys, interviews, usability testing, and ethnographic observation to gather data and digital tools like Google Analytics and A/B testing platforms to analyze user behavior. My strengths include synthesizing research data, translating findings into actionable recommendations, and effectively communicating my findings to stakeholders.

User Empathy

My approach to user empathy involves a deep dive into their world through methods such as interviews and ethnographic observation. For example, while working on an e-commerce platform, I discovered users felt overwhelmed by the amount of product options and lacked personalized recommendations. To address these issues, I designed a recommendation system using machine learning and simplified the interface. I use tools like persona creation and have a background in human-centered design principles and design thinking methodologies. Overall, I'm committed to understanding user needs and using those insights to design products that truly meet them.

Qualitative Research

My approach to qualitative interviews involves building rapport with users and asking open-ended questions that encourage them to share their thoughts and experiences. For example, while working on a travel app, I asked users open-ended questions like "Can you describe a time when you had a negative experience booking a trip?" to gain insights into their needs and pain points. I listen actively and use follow-up questions to uncover hidden insights that may not have surfaced with closed-ended questions. Overall, my commitment to understanding user experiences and perspectives shapes my approach to qualitative interviews.
